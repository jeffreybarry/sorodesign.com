BOOK COVERS, BOOK INTERIORS, & AUTHOR WEBSITES

CECILIA SOROCHIN:
Book Designer – An ever-present dedication to design & love for books makes Ceci passionate for her work. When she found that she could make a living by designing books, she felt like “finding love”. Graduating as a graphic designer from the Universidad de Palermo, she taught typography at Universidad de Palermo and Universidad de Buenos Aires. Before turning to book design, she studied fashion design at Universidad de Buenos Aires and worked in the fashion industry for many years.
ceci@sorodesign.com
 

JEFF BARRY:
Tech Guy– A librarian by profession, on the internet since 1990 & creating web sites since 1993, starting with online exhibitions for the Library of Congress. He served as head of systems development for the Old Dominion University Libraries and then as director of the digital library at the University of Miami. Jeff has published over a dozen articles on technology and made conference presentations in the Bahamas, England, India, Trinidad, and the United States. Jeff also blogs at buenos aires, city of faded elegance.
jeff@sorodesign.com
