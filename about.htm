CECILIA SOROCHIN
Book Designer – An ever-present dedication to design & love for books makes Ceci passionate for her work. Book design has changed her life. When she found that she could make a living by designing books, she felt like “finding love”. She believes book design is a discourse where white space, letters and images play a part on each page to tell a story. Graduating as a graphic designer from the University of Palermo, she taught typography at University of Palermo and University of Buenos Aires.
